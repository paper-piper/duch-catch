# Game Concept & Design
our game will feature a duck trying eat Bread. the player will control the duck moving only left and right and try to catch the falling bread that will spawn. on the top left we will have a count of how many bread did the duck ate (alongside with a bread icon to indicate that its bread count), and on the right we will have a timer showing how much time does the player has left starting with one minute (alongside with a timer icon).

## Game Design
the whole game will be in pixel art, and will contain the following assets:
- Duck sprite: idle and another sprite opening his mouth and preparing to eat. both sprite will feature the duck looking up. (bonus: a sprite of the duck walking and a sprite of the duck swallowing the bread)
- Bread sprite: a simple rotated bread image to make it interesting (bonus: particales of the bread falling and varius angles of the bread)
- Icons: Timer icon and bread icon, to be displayed on the menu
- Background: a sunny day with a nice lake in the background.
